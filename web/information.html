How can I register ?

In order to register you must follow the steps below
  1. You must have a username, with which you will enter the web application.
  2. You need to enter your full name.
  3. You must have an email, in which an account verification email will be sent to you.
  4. You need to put your phone number or conventional number for the customer to contact you.
